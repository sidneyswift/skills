# Loose notes (to be filed by the system)

- **Recurring question** clients ask: "What's your standard discovery deliverable?" Our answer: a
  two-week diagnostic, a findings readout deck, and a 90-day pricing roadmap.

- **Recurring question:** "Do you offer any guarantee?" Yes — a satisfaction guarantee on the first
  (diagnostic) phase: if the readout isn't useful, they don't pay for that phase.

- **Decision (2026-05):** we standardized on **value-based pricing** proposals over cost-plus. Reason:
  higher close value, more defensible, and it matches how clients actually talk about ROI. We don't
  pitch cost-plus engagements anymore.

- **Reusable proposal structure** we use every time: (1) exec summary, (2) diagnosis of the problem,
  (3) our approach, (4) scope & deliverables, (5) pricing, (6) timeline, (7) the guarantee, (8) about
  us / proof. We rebuild this from scratch each time, which is annoying.

- **One-off idea:** someday it might be cool to build an interactive pricing calculator for prospects.
  Not now — just parking the thought.
