# Root-cause reframing reference

## The two layers

Every productizable problem has two versions:

| Layer | What it is | Role in the product |
|---|---|---|
| **Surface problem** | What the person *thinks* their problem is | Marketing — this is what gets attention |
| **Root problem** | What's *actually* going on underneath | Product — this is what delivers transformation |

You sell the surface. You solve the root. Miss either and the product fails:
sell the root and nobody recognizes the problem. Solve only the surface and
the results are shallow — no transformation, no testimonials, no retention.

## How to find the root

### The "why" ladder

Take the surface problem and ask "why is that a problem?" repeatedly:

```
Surface: "I need more clients"
Why? → "I don't have enough revenue"
Why? → "I can't predict my income month to month"
Why? → "I don't have a system that reliably brings clients to me"
Root: "I don't know how to position myself so ideal clients seek me out"
```

Usually 3-5 "why" steps reach the root. Stop when the answer becomes
something the person needs to *learn* or *build*, not just *do more of*.

### The "what did you actually change" test

Ask: "When you solved this, what was the actual thing you changed?"

The surface version describes symptoms. The root version describes the
structural change that fixed those symptoms.

```
Surface: "I wasn't getting replies to my cold emails"
What changed: "I stopped pitching features and started leading with the
prospect's specific problem"
Root: "I didn't understand my prospect's pain well enough to lead with it"
```

### The "everyone tries this first" test

Ask: "What does everyone try first that doesn't work?"

The thing people try first is usually the surface-level fix. The thing
that actually works targets the root.

```
Everyone tries: "Send more emails" (volume solution to a surface problem)
What works: "Rewrite 3 lines of your email to lead with their pain"
(precision solution to the root problem)
```

## Common surface → root patterns

| Surface | Root |
|---|---|
| "I need more followers" | "I don't have a content strategy that compounds" |
| "I need more clients" | "I can't articulate my value clearly enough for the right people to find me" |
| "I need to lose weight" | "I don't have a sustainable system — I rely on willpower" |
| "My business is stuck" | "I'm doing everything myself instead of building systems" |
| "I can't find time" | "I haven't identified and eliminated my lowest-value activities" |
| "Nobody opens my emails" | "My list doesn't trust me because I haven't delivered value first" |
